Keeping Up and Staying Current

There are a couple different ways you can be sure to stay as current as possible on what's happening in the PEZ world. First and foremost, check out the New Dispensers section of this site. I'll be keeping this page updated on a regular basis.

Join the Mailing List
Second, join the Pezheads Mailing List...a group of over 600(!) people that can send one message, and have it read by everyone subscribed! This free service is where you'll find all the late breaking news, hot deals on PEZ sales, PEZ happenings in the news, and really any and everything else that is related to PEZ. The Pezheads Mailing List has is a private email list. To sign up, Please mail Karen Cooper <karenc@visi.com> with your request to "join the pezheads list". She will send you message with information on how to join.

There is also a pez newsgroup, but it doesn't seem to get used too much. When you have such a great mailing list, there seems no need for one. But just so you know, it's alt.food.pez. As an alternative to this newsgroup, try checking out my PEZ forum. It works much like a newsgroup, except you can include links and images easier, and the posts will remain available.
