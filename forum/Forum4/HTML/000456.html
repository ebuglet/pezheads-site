pezsue posted 10-17-00 08:36 PM CT (US)            
I display my pez in 2 ways. I have 2 small wooden display "tracks" that hold about 10 dispensers each. I use thse on my desk at work and each month I change them out for the theme of the month. Last month was cats and it ran the gamut from pink panther to Garfield to Sylvester to Roar the Lion. The month before was swimmers with sharks, octpus, frog, turtles etc. This gives me a chance to really appreciate and enjoy my collection in a creative way and share it with visitors to my office. My best dispensers are mixed right in there with current dispensers. As a CPA I get lots of visitors. I also have a stash of new dispensers to give to kids when they leave so it's a motivational tool as well. Who knows who will be a collector because I got them going?

On the other hand my collection at home is mine to enjoy so all items are displayed loose on acrylic stadium displays. I tried one with a cover to circumvent the inevitable dust demon but it gave me no joy to see the glare or reflection rather than those happy pez heads so I tossed the cover out. I also have special truck displays. One display hols 36 trucks. The idea is to maximize display and minimize space. You can display about 100 dispensers in about 2 feet of shelf space. Sometimes I do get a duplicate and they are stored away by year to be sold in 5 yrs time to finance further collection efforts. I currently display about 400 dispensers in a non-obnoxious way. Jeesh-it't embarrassing enough.
