brianr posted 05-22-01 01:04 PM CT (US)         
The eyebrows sound like sloppy painting by the machine - that is less common now, but in earlier dispensers the paint was so sloppy that no two were exactly the same - if you wanted to collect "variations" based on sloppy paint you would need basicly every dispenser ever produced of some series (like the old lambs - I have a dozen and no 2 are the same). So that sounds like a slight machine varient rather than a full on collectable variation like the line/no line on eeore. The lack of grey on the hinge area of Tigger sounds interesting - I wonder if it is a common thing or if you found a factory error (some people collect errors, but usually only if still in package.
