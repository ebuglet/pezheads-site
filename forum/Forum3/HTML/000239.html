feldamundo posted 02-19-00 10:19 PM CT (US)            
I suppose that the Los Angeles convention next month and the national St. Louis convention are the two biggest shows, but any PEZ convention is worth attending. You will never see more PEZ in one place than you will at a convention, and you can score some pretty good bargains there, too. It's also nice to see many of the rarest dispensers and PEZ items in-person and meet other collectors from around the world. If you can afford to make the trip to any of the conventions, I would definitely recommend going.
