pezjenny posted 10-29-99 10:11 PM CT (US)        
I am on the web now, doing my daily check of Pez websites for the latest Pez news and such, and was just thinking about how glad I am that others are so nice to post on the web and keep stuff updated - it is what really help me enjoy the hobby.

So here are my specific kudos - first, to Jaime, for this website, which I adore - for this forum, the database, the links. In my mind, the best Pez resource on the net.

Secondly, to Michel, the Pezpimp, <www.globalserve.net/~pezcondo/> for maintaining the fantastic new updates page, which is where I get all of my hot new Pez news. I check his page daily - it is THE place to get the latest Pez gossip.

Thanks to you both. and please, fellow Pezheads, feel free to add some other thank yous. I know this hobby wouldn't be so much fun for me if it wasn't for the work people put into maintaining such good quality Pez webpages.

pezjenny
