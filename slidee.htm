PEZ Slidee Puzzle!

Instructions:

After clicking on the puzzle above, the picture will be broken up into 16 squares and scrambled. The gray square is the "empty" one...click on the square that you'd like to move to the empty spot. Keep doing so until you put the puzzle back together. You can not move diagonally. Enjoy..happy pezzing!

Click on the game above to begin.

 System Requirements:

  • A browser that supports Shockwave plug-ins...get Netscape already!
  • If you get an "Error loading Director Move -- Out of Memory" error, try allocating more memory to Netscape...this will fix your problem.
  • You'll also need the Shockwave plugin for Director 5...follow the links until you find the one that fits your machine.
  • If you can't view the whole game on your screen... using Netscape, you can hide the toolbar. Choose the "Options" menu and deselect "Show Toolbar", "Show Location", and "Show Directory Buttons".
