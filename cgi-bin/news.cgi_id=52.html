Availability: They're Out!

Update: Officially scheduled to be released on June 1st, but I got mine already! Pretty cool dispensers, come in regular U.S. polybag and you gotta have a food purchase to buy them!


Jack in the Box fast food restaurants are planning to release a Jack pez! I contacted them and they said they plan to advertise them (I've already spotted one in a commercial) and will release them in several months. They will probably come on 3 to 4 different stem colors.
