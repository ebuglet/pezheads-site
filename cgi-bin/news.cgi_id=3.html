Availability: Were released during mid November, 1997.. Not in the US.

My review...ehh..kind of a disappointment. I think the detail is lacking compared to the older smurfs, and ESPECIALLY compared to the Pink Panther series. I would have also liked to have seen Brainy's glasses as actual glasses, and not just painted on. More like the Raven. One last thing.. although I really like the neon stem colors that PEZ Candy, Inc has taken to using, I liked the olders dispensers that actually had matching stems better. (Papa smurf has the bright neon orage stem, but a red hat.)
