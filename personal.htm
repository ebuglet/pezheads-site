Personal Collections
NOTICE: With the unveiling of the PEZhead Locator I may be abandoning this page. The PEZhead Locator integrates the collection photos with each person's personal bio information. To search the PEZhead database for all people with photos click below.

These are pictures of personal collections of pezheads all over the world. Often times when you start collecting, one of the hardest things to do is find a good way to display your PEZ. Hopefully by seeing what others have done, it will help you come up with an idea of your own. I'd like to get as many different collections up as possible. If you'd like your collection up, drop me a note and I'll let you know where to send the photographs. Just think... it's a free way to have your PEZ collection scanned and placed on the internet! Anyway, click on the images for the bigger picture.
