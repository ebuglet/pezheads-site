System Requirements:

  • A browser that supports Shockwave plug-ins...get Netscape already!
  • I recommend that you have a PPC with at least 16meg of RAM, or you will find the game to run extremely slow. Also make sure your browser's cache is set high. ALSO TRY GIVING NETSCAPE MORE MEMORY!
  • You'll also need the Shockwave plugin for Director 5...follow the links until you find the one that fits your machine.
  • If you can't view the whole game on your screen... using Netscape, you can hide the toolbar. Choose the "Options" menu and deselect "Show Toolbar", "Show Location", and "Show Directory Buttons".
